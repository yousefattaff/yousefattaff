  About Me
</h2>
Hi! I'm Yousef — an AI Software Engineer from Cairo 🇪🇬, passionate about building real-world intelligent systems powered by large language models, autonomous agents, and RAG pipelines. I went through Harvard's CS50 and CS50 AI, and completed Ed Donner's full LLM Engineering curriculum on Udemy.
🤖 Building with Frontier & Open-Source LLMs (GPT, Claude, Gemini, LLaMA)
🔗 Hands-on with LangChain, LangGraph, CrewAI, AutoGen & MCP
🧠 Experienced in QLoRA fine-tuning, RAG, and vector databases
📚 Harvard CS50x & CS50 AI graduate
⚡ Strong low-level foundations in C and bare-metal embedded systems
🌍 Based in Cairo, Egypt — open to remote opportunities
